About Us

Feed your cravings with your favorite foods delivered to you, wherever you are. From meals to desserts, our wide selection of food will definitely satisfy all your food cravings. Red Bird is a food delivery service that connects the Red Bird restaurant to the people. When you place an order with Red Bird , we receive it and assign a delivery partner to pick up the order from the restaurant to bring it to you.

We are a small team of problem solvers, designers, thinkers and tinkers, working around the clock to make Red Bird a powerful food delivery system. We believe that ordering food should be easy, fast and definitely fun!
