Features

Menu

Are you looking for food to eat? Browse through our menu that lists all the dishes Red Bird can offer! You can navigate through various dish types such as: The Originals, Bundles, Breakfast Meals, Beverages, Desserts, and Extras.

Delivery

Want something to eat while watching Netflix? Worry no more! Have Red Bird's tasty dishes delivered in front of your doorstep. Simply fill up your cart and create an order. In a matter of minutes, you can then enjoy your food and movie without going out!

Online Payment

Red Bird orders are made more convenient through contactless payments! Payments may be done using various mobile wallets such as GCash and other bank applications.

Cart

Don't know what you're craving for? You may choose to order later and put all your favorite Red Bird dishes in your cart! So whenever you decided to choose Red Bird, a click will be all you need!
